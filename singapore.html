SINGAPORE

Singapore tour package

Day 1 - Arrival in Singapore

Welcome to Singapore! Upon arrival at the Changi airport, clear the immigration formalities and get transferred to the hotel.Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 2 - Singapore - Half Day Singapore Panoramic City Drive & Sentosa Island Tour.

After breakfast, proceed for a Half day Singapore City Tour including Suntec City, Fountain of Wealth, Orchard Road, Little India and Chinatown. By afternoon, you will be dropped to Sentosa island. Take the cable car from Mount Faber & get entry into sentosa at Imbiah station. take photos with Merlion statue. Enjoy the most amazing & must do Sky & Luge ride. Later in evening get ready to enjoy the lazer & light show with the help of water fountain called Wings of time.Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 3 - Singapore - Universal Studios.

After Breakfast, Get Ready to go beyond the screen and Ride The Movies™ at Universal Studios Singapore™. Experience cutting-edge rides, shows, and attractions based on your favorite blockbuster films and television series, including Puss In Boots’ Giant Journey, Battlestar Galactica : HUMAN vs. CYLON™, TRANSFORMERS The Ride : The Ultimate 3D Battle, Jurassic Park Rapids Adventure™, Sesame Street Spaghetti Space Chase and more! Universal Studios Singapore™ is sure to indulge the thrill-seeker or movie buff's every fantasy.Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 4 - Day at Leisure

After breakfast, your day is at leisure, you can go for shopping or any other activity. Go & explore the botanical gardens of Singapore, enjoy the street food at novena district or explore the nightlife at Clark Quay area.Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 5 - Departure from Singapore

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and proceed to the airport for your return flight to India.
