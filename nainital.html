NAINITAL

NAINITAL package

DAY 01: DELHI – NAINITAL BY ROAD (310KMS / 8HRS)

Pick up from Delhi airport and transfers to Nainital, upon arrival at Nainital check in to the hotel. Rest of the day at leisure. In the evening visit Naini Devi Temple, Tibetan Market, Mall road Naina Devi Temple and Thandi Sadak and dinner overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 02: NAINITAL

After Breakfast proceed for lake tour (Bhimtal, Sattal, Nakuchiatal, Fruit Market and Bhowali) then back to Nainital, enjoy one Row Boat Ride in Naini Lake then Back to the hotel, overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 03: NAINITAL – KAUSANI BY ROAD (120 KMS/ 5 HRS.)

After breakfast, drive to Kausani via Almora. Kausani is ideal for those who seek to escape from the din and dust, hustle and bustle of big cities and wish to spend a quiet holiday in the lap of nature. Check in Hotel. After fresh up visit Sun set at View Point, Gandhi Ji Ashram. Dinner at Hotel. Over Night Stay in Hotel.

DAY 04: KAUSANI

Day at Leisure or visit local sightseeing i.e. Dinner at Hotel. Over Night Stay in Hotel.

DAY 05: KAUSANI – JIM CORBETT BY ROAD (190KM/6HRS)

After Breakfast check out from the hotel and transfers to Jim Corbett National Park. Upon arrival check-in to your resort and enjoy indoor games and rest of the day the leisure. Overnight stay at resort.

DAY 06: JIM CORBETT DEPARTURE BY ROAD (240 KMS/5 HRS.)

Early Morning move for Safari Ride (optional cost), enjoy your Jungle Safari and back to hotel for breakfast. Check out from the resort and transfers to Delhi airport to catch the flight for onward destination.
