SHIMLA

Shimla tour package

DAY 1: DRIVE TO SHIMLA (350 KMS / APPROX. 8 HOURS)

Welcome to Delhi - Meet & greet by our representative upon arrival and transfers to Shimla. Shimla- The small hill station amidst the lofty snow-clad Himalayan ranges, surrounded by lakes and lush green pastures. Upon arrival at Shimla, Check-in at the hotel and spend evening at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay in Shimla.

DAY 2: EXCURSION TOUR OF KUFRI

After breakfast drive for excursion tour of Kufri. This is a popular picnic spot located 16km from Shimla. You may hike on foot to the Mahasu Peak or pay for a mule ride up the hill. Mahasu is the tallest peak in Kufri. Return to the hotel in the afternoon. In the evening, take a walk on Shimla’s famous boulevard, Mall Road. See fine specimens of colonial architecture, snack at the various eateries in the market or shop for souvenirs. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 3: SHIMLA – MANALI (250 KMS / APPROX. 6 HOURS)

After breakfast checkout from the hotel and will drive towards Manali – Offers super views of the Dhauladhar and Pir Panjal ranges, and with mountain adventures beckoning from all directions, Manali is a year-round magnet for tourists. Manali is popular among adventure tourists coming for trekking, paragliding, rafting and skiing. Check-in at the hotel and spend evening at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay in Manali.

DAY 4: SIGHTSEEING IN MANALI

In morning after breakfast, go on an adventurous drive to Solang valley – a major attraction, without which the trip remains incomplete. All above mentioned adventure activities can be availed here at Solang Valley (Optional cost). Apart from activities, there are splendid views to enjoy in and around the Solang Valley. Later, today visit popular Hadimba Temple, Naggar Castle and Art gallery. In the evening, go for a walk along Mall Road. Dinner & Overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 05 MANALI – CHANDIGARH (300 KMS / APPROX. 6.5 HOURS)

After breakfast, drive towards Chandigarh and check-in at the hotel. If time permits, visit Nek Chand's Rock Garden which consists of man-made interlinked waterfalls and many other sculptures that have been made of scrap & other kinds of wastes materials. Overnight stay in Chandigarh.

DAY 06 CHANDIGARH - DELHI (245 KMS / APPROX. 5 HOURS)

After breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Delhi (250 kms/5.5 hrs.). Arrive Delhi and transfer to Airport to connect onward flight.
