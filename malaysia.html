Malaysia package

Day 1 : Arrive in Kualalumpur

On arrival you will be met by our local representative in the arrival area situated outside the customs hall. Later continue your journey to Genting Highlands – a beautiful mountain resort and the ‘city of entertainment’. We then proceed to the Genting Highland's Cable Car Station. This is the longest cable car in South East Asia – a distance of 3.4 kms. On arrival at the 'top' we proceed to the Hotel. Check into your rooms and relax. After Check inn we will visit the casino (there is a dress code for men and 21 years and below are not allowed inside). This casino is open for 24 hours. After the orientation tour – you are free. Overnight at Genting Highlands.

Day 2 : Batu Cave

After an International buffet breakfast at your hotel, In the afternoon we proceed to Kuala Lumpur. Enroute visit Batu Caves also known as the Sri Subramaniam Temple Complex one of the most visited tourism spots in Malaysia. We spend approximately 1 hour here. Overnight in Hotel Central Market Kualalumpur.

Day 3 : City Tour

After continental breakfast at your hotel, In the afternoon we proceed for Kualalumpur City tour to visit the observatory deck of the famous Menara KL Tower for a 360 degree view of this beautiful city. Continue on a guided tour of this bustling city. Visit the National Museum (not inclusive of the entrance fee), see the King's Palace (photo stop) residence of the ruler of Malaysia, The National Mosque (photo stop), the National monument, Cocoa Boutique,Sultan Abdul Samad Building and the superb buildings at the Independence square Make a photo stop of the magnificent Petronas Twin Towers, one of the tallest buildings in the world. after that we are proceed Aquaria KLCC, located on the concourse level of the Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre, is said to be one of the largest aquariums in Southeast Asia. Overnight in Hotel Central Market Kualalumpur.

Day 4 :

Enjoy an continental breakfast at your hotel. Later today, proceed to the airport as this fantastic tour comes to an end. Return home with wonderful memories of your holiday with us.
