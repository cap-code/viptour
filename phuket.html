Phuket and krabi

Phuket and krabi tour package

Day 1 - Arrival

Arrive at the airport and transfer to Phuket. Complete the check-in formalities and stay overnight at the resort. (For Evening Suggested: Phuket FantaSea Show with Dinner - A show with more than 150 artists and live animals like elephants and horses and History of the Siam)

Day 2 - Phi Phi Island Tour by Big Boat

Enjoy breakfast at your hotel. Get ready for a morning trip to the Phi Phi Islands. The pickup time is 7:15 AM from the hotel lobby. It is advised that you wear shorts, T Shirt and slippers and keep your valuables at the hotel's safe deposit. Enjoy beach activities and lunch on the island. Spend your evening exploring the flea markets. Retire for the day at your hotel.

Day 3 - Explore Phuket

Enjoy Breakfast at Hotel. After Breakfast, rest of the day is free for Shopping and Self Exploration. Overnight at Hotel

Day 4 - Phuket to Krabi

Enjoy Breakfast in the hotel. After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Krabi in the comfort of a private vehicle. Upon arrival at Krabi, head to the beautiful resort and complete the check-in formalities. Enjoy beach activities and the pool in the resort. Stay overnight in the resort.

Day 5 - 4 Island Tour by Longtail Boat

Enjoy breakfast in the hotel. Get ready for a morning trip to the 4 Islands. The pickup time is 10:00 Am from the hotel lobby. It is recommended that you wear shorts, T-shirt and slippers. Keep your valuables in the hotel's safe deposit. Enjoy beach activities on the island and also enjoy a delicious lunch. Spend your evening at leisure exploring the flea markets. Stay overnight at the hotel.

Day 6 - Explore Krabi

Eat a lip-smacking breakfast at the hotel and then set out to explore Krabi.

Day 7 - Goodbye Thailand

Eat a hearty breakfast at the hotel and then check-out from the hotel and take transfers to the airport to catch your flight back home.
